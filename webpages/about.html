What is SplitMate?

SplitMate is an application that makes it easy to split any kind of bill with friends and family. Your information on spending patterns and shared expenses are easily managed in this app, ensuring you know how much you're spending and who owes who.
Whether it's a household bill with flatmates, or a family outing for dinner, SplitMate makes your financial life easier.
